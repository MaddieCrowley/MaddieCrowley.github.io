Equipment Repair

I have, as it is my job, repaired a vast array of different equipment. Some of my current and past work includes, but is not limited to:
  • Various Keithley electrometers and picoammeters
  • All sorts of bench-top power-supplies
  • Various analog and digital oscilloscopes
  • Various NIM modules
  • Audio receivers and amplifiers
  • Scientific filters
  • Various parts of a squeeze caster
  • Ultra-high vacuum equipment
  • Helium compressors
  • Frequency counters
  • Function generators
  • Lock-in amplifiers
  • And much more!
At some point, I intend to make a writeup of all the different stuff I have fixed, but for now this page will suffice.
